Your reality

The 3 headaches of affiliate marketplaces.

01Entrance is gated.

Publishers have limited "shelf space" already filled with big brand names. Securing shelf space only works if you know what they're optimizing for.

02Opaque feedback.

You're in, but not getting enough customers and not getting useful information from the publisher. You sense something's wrong, but can't tell if it's your product, the funnel, the underwriting, or the relationship. You spend the next 6–12 months guessing what to change.

03Silent delisting.

Publishers are objective and data-driven in their decision making. When your ads don't perform, they may run your ads on low-quality inventory or, worse, completely remove you — often silently. If you burn a channel, you're iced for 2–3 years.

The hardest part

Most lenders don't know where to find a playbook to address these 3 challenges.

Why DIY fails

The answer won't come from inside your org.

Affiliate marketplaces are fiercely competitive. They have strong POVs on product, application funnels, integrations, APIs, conversion rates. The list goes on.

To win, your entire organization needs to be aligned on what needs to be delivered, the reasons behind it, and the ROI on those investments.

At this point you have two options:

Debate internally for 12+ months.

  • Credit, product, engineering, and marketing argue about what matters.
  • Nobody in the room has seen this before.
  • Even if you luck into alignment, you still have 6–12 months of execution ahead.
  • Realistic timeline: two years.
  • Meanwhile you're burning budget and possibly burning the channel.

Bring in operators who've seen this 50+ times.

  • Cross-functional alignment in 60 days.
  • Improvements to conversion rates in 90.
  • Significant volume increases in 120.
  • Execute your operating plan with conviction in the ROI.
